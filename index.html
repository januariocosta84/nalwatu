Our Story

Technology has been starting to evolve in Timor Leste since the 1990s.

Back in those times, very limited people had access to technology such as computers, the internet, and mobile phones.

After Timor Leste's independence in 1999, the majority of technology was brought to the country by the United Nations Mission.

Most of the work done in many Government Institutions, Non-Government Institutions, Private sectors, and Society is heavily dependent on technology.

Although technology is seen as one of the solutions to boost social and economic development in Timor Leste, there are quite a large number of organizations that still have difficulties in utilizing technology properly to gain their business outcomes.

Government and organizations still highly depend on IT support and services from outside the country, which leaves quite a large number of new IT students out of jobs.

NALWATU DALINEO UNIPESSOAL LDA was founded by Mr. Elisio Mariano Fraga Freitas as Director, with two other Engineers and six IT Specialists.

This company commenced its operation since October 2017.

The mission of this company is to close the emerging gap in Timor Leste in the IT Industry.
